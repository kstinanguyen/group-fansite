Fun facts

What Are Invisible Pets?

  • Invisible pets are companions you can't see but can feel! They’re perfect for anyone with a wild imagination who’s ready to adopt a one-of-a-kind, low-maintenance friend. They can be anything: invisible dogs, cats, dragons, or even something entirely unique to the owner.
  • Invisible pets don’t shed, bark, meow, or require a litter box!

Meet Our Most Popular Breeds

  • Phantomo Cats: Known for their silent purrs, they keep you company without knocking things over.
  • Breezy Beagles: Experts at invisible fetch games, and known for sniffing out imaginary treats.
  • Cloud Dragons: They hover near you like a soft breeze and love to "breathe" invisible fire.

Invisible Pet Care 101

  • Feeding: Invisible pets have refined tastes, but fortunately, they love imaginary food! Just pretend to pour it out, and they’ll be thrilled.
  • Exercise: Invisible dogs love long, pretend walks, while invisible cats enjoy imaginary sunbeams.
  • Bathing: Baths are easy—just pretend to wash them, and they’ll be clean in no time!
